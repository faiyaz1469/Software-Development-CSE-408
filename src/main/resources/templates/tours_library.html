A Tremendous Experience in Bogalake

Sayem Al Hasan

BogaLake is a wonderful natural lake known for its scenic beauty, 65km from Bandarban and 20 km from Ruma Thana. This overnight trip involves a two hour drive to Painkhyongchori, a boat journey to Ruma bazaar and a five hour trek to BogaLake. Your local guide will arrange the overnight stay and all meals. You will need to carry a sleeping bag along with your essentials.
